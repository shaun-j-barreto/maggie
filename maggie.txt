Maggie Ingredients
==================
1) Maggie Noddles
2) Onions
3) Tomatoes
4) Water
5) Oil
6) Spring onions
7) chili oil

Procedure:
1) Chop onions, tomatoes and spring onions
2) Put oil in the pan and saute the onions until they are golden brown
3) Just before onions are done, add tomatoes
4) Boil nooddles as per packet instructions and keep it aside...keep some of the water to use as a thickener.
5) Now add the sautaed onions and tomatoes to the nooddles, mix well
6) Cook on a low flame, stir well.
7) Add garnish including, chopped spring onions and chili
8) Serve it in a medium bowl..Enjoy
